VEGGIE FARMHOUSE PIZZA

THE VERY best (freezer-friendly) Farmhouse Pizza!

So rich, so hearty, so perfect.







Pizza is the holy grail of all comfort foods and anyone who doesn't

agree with this statement, is not a true foodie!! This recipe is pretty easy

and results in the most delicious, crisp pizzas loaded with a variety of vegetables.

PIZZA

yield: 4 SERVINGS

prep: 20 MINUTES

cook: 35 MINUTES

total: 55 MINUTES

THE VERY perfect Farmhouse Pizza! So rich, so tender, so heartwarming.

With exiqiuite cuisine.

TRULY A ONE SKILLET DINNER! So so easy!

4.5 stars(2 ratings)




Ingredients


  • 2 cups plain flour
  • ½ cup luke-warm water
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp dry yeast
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1-2 tbsn water for kneading, if needed
  • For the Tomato & Basil Pizza sauce
  • 2 red tomatoes
  • 4 tbsn passata or 2 tbsn tomato pure
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dry & mixed Italian herbs
  • 1 tbsp tomato sauce


Instructions


    Firstly, let us activate the yeast for the dough

    Next, add your flour, salt and oil

    Dust some flour on the kitchen platform and knead your dough very well for 8-9 minutes to activate gluten

    3.Grease a deep bowl with oil and place your dough in it. Cover with a cling wrap.

    Rest the wrapped bowl of dough in a warm place for 2 hours.

    In the meantime, prepare your pizza sauce. In a saucepan, heat some olive oil.

    Add the chopped garlic and basil leaves. Saute for a few seconds.

    Add the blanched tomatoes. Mix and cook covered for 4-5 minutes on medium-low flame.

    Take the risen flour and punch the dough lightly with fist to release down the air.

    Place one doughball on a dusted baking paper. With a rolling pin, flatten the dough-ball into a medium-sized pizza crust.

    Top with your colorful veggie toppings, salt, pepper and mozzarella cheese.

    Bake your pizza for 12-15 minutes. Cut into slices and enjoy!
